# Community Code of Conduct

Nyxbyte should be a welcoming project for curious builders, artists, desktop
customization fans, and first-time open-source contributors.

## Our standards

Please:

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive.
- Critique ideas and code without attacking people.
- Welcome questions and different levels of experience.
- Credit artwork, code, and ideas that are not your own.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, spam, and sexualized conduct.

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit or remove contributions that violate these
standards and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when
necessary. Reports should be sent privately to the repository owner through
GitHub.

By participating, you agree to follow these standards in repository issues,
pull requests, discussions, and other project spaces.
